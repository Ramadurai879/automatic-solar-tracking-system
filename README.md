# ☀️ Automatic Solar Tracking System

## 📌 Overview

The **Automatic Solar Tracking System** is an Arduino-based renewable energy project that automatically aligns a solar panel with the sun to maximize solar energy absorption. The system uses **Light Dependent Resistor (LDR)** sensors to detect sunlight intensity and controls a **servo motor** to continuously adjust the panel's position. This improves power generation compared to fixed solar panels.

## 🚀 Features

* Automatic solar panel tracking
* LDR-based sunlight detection

## ⚙️ Working Principle

Two LDR sensors continuously monitor sunlight intensity. The Arduino compares the sensor readings and determines the direction of the strongest light. A servo motor then rotates the solar panel toward the brighter side until both sensors receive nearly equal light, ensuring the panel remains aligned with the sun throughout the day.
